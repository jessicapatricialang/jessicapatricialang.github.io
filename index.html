I am currently a PhD researcher at European University Institute in Florence, Italy. Additionally, I serve as the Chair of the ECPR Standing Group on Participation and Mobilisation, and co-coordinate the Environmental and Climate Politics Working Group hosted at EUI. My research interest revolves around the ways democratic societies make sense of environmental crises such as climate change or biodiversity loss and develop a hegemonic understanding of legitimate ways to address them.

On this website, you can find out about my research, publications, teaching, and CV. Feel free to reach out at jessica.lang[at]eui.eu in case you would like to know more!
