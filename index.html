I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Zurich. My research revolves around the ways democratic societies make sense of environmental degradation, such as climate change and biodiversity loss, and develop a hegemonic understanding of legitimate ways to address them. Additionally, I serve as the Chair of the ECPR Standing Group on Participation and Mobilisation.

I hold a PhD in Political and Social Sciences from the European University Institute in Florence, Italy. During my PhD, I was a visiting researcher at the WZB Berlin Social Science Center, the University of Zurich, and the University of Gothenburg.

On this website, you can find out more about my research, publications, and teaching, and download my CV. Feel free to get in touch at jessica.lang [at] ipz.uzh.ch.
